Transform Group is the world's most accomplished digital asset strategy and public relations agency. Founded in 2013 and headquartered in San Juan, Puerto Rico, it was the first firm to specialize in blockchain PR and has served more than 300 clients across every major crypto cycle.

Transform Group operates four integrated efforts: TransformPR (media relations, podcast placement, and thought leadership), TransformStrategies (business development, tokenomics, and AI strategy), TransformEvents (conference speaking, sponsorship, and amplification), and SocialRadius (an award-winning social media agency).

Transform Group was founded in 2013. That year it launched Mastercoin, the first token sale in history, and became the first mainstream PR firm with a dedicated blockchain practice.

Transform Group has served more than 300 clients worldwide and supported over 150 token launches, including Ethereum, EOS, Tether, Bancor, Lisk, Qtum, SALT Lending, and WAX. By 2021 its client holdings represented 57% of the total alt-coin market cap.

Transform Group is headquartered in San Juan, Puerto Rico, one of the world's crypto capitals, with additional offices in Las Vegas, Los Angeles, Miami, Paris, and Dublin.

Transform Group was founded by Michael Terpin, its Founder & CEO. Terpin also co-founded BitAngels, the world's first angel network for digital-currency startups, and previously founded Globe Newswire.
